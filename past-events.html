Past Events

The previous event in this conference series, RTESE, was held on August 23-25, 2017, in Toronto, Canada. Our year-long planning and organization of several committees resulted in the conference becoming a major success. Please see below some of the highlights of the event.

Best Paper Award Winners

We would like to congratulate the following authors for winning the Best Paper Award:

  • Gülten Yüksek
    for the paper entitled: "Laccase-Mediator System for the Elimination of Carbamazepine and Atrazine"
  • Dr. Jeong Hwan Kim
    for the paper entitled: "Higher Selective Ion Transportation Using Functional Metal Coated PTFE Membrane with Electrical Bias"
  • Dr. Mariola Kądziołka-Gaweł
    for the paper entitled: "Characterization of Steel Production Dusts: Mössbauer Spectroscopy Study"
  • Dr. Nozar Samani
    for the paper entitled: "Climate Change Trend in Fars Province, Iran and Its Effect on Groundwater Crisis"
