Best Paper Award Winners

We would like to congratulate the following authors for winning the Best Paper Award:

  • Aikaterini Kalogiannidou
    for the paper entitled: "Generation and Composition of Medical Waste Derived From Histopathology Laboratories"
  • Seungjin Oh
    for the paper entitled: "The Feasibility Study on Biodrying for Solid Refuse Fuel (SRF) of Municipal Sewage Sludge"

    for the paper entitled: "Review of Applications and Environmental Fate of Gold Nanoparticles"
