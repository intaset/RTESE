We are pleased to announce that the 2nd International Conference of Recent Trends in Environmental Science and Engineering (RTESE'18) will be held in Niagara Falls, Canada, on June 10 - 12, 2018 at the Americana Resort.

The City of Niagara Falls is a small vibrant city located in the heart of the Niagara Region. Where else can you enjoy all the amenities of a large urban centre in a comfortable, lively community? Whatever you are looking for you can find it in Niagara Falls – Arts, culture, recreation, commerce and peaceful quiet moments – they’re all in Niagara Falls.

The Americana Resort is an excellent Niagara Falls hotel located minutes from the Falls. Whether planning the perfect family vacation, an important business trip or a successful meeting or conference – the Americana has the perfect room for you.

The Americana Resort is an excellent conference hotel with quick highway access from either direction coming from Toronto, Ontario or from Buffalo, USA. Getting there is easy!
